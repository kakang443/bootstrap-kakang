Siquijor Port

The Siquijor Port, located in the municipality of Siquijor (the capital town of Siquijor Island), serves as one of the island’s main entry points for both locals and tourists. Positioned along the southwestern coast, the port is relatively small but well-maintained, catering primarily to passenger ferries that connect the island to nearby destinations such as Dumaguete, Tagbilaran, and occasionally Cebu. Ferries operated by companies like OceanJet, Montenegro Lines, and Anika Star make daily trips to and from the port, making it a convenient and accessible travel hub. The port is within walking distance of the town center, and visitors arriving here are often greeted by scenic ocean views and a peaceful, laid-back atmosphere that reflects the island's charm. Unlike the larger and busier Larena Port, Siquijor Port is preferred by travelers seeking a more direct route to the island’s capital or nearby beach resorts.
