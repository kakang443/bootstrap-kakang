Sawang Spot

The Larena Fish Port, situated in Barangay Taculing, Larena, Siquijor, serves as a vital hub for both local fishermen and travelers. Beyond its role in fish landing and trading, it also functions as a passenger port, connecting Siquijor to neighboring islands like Dumaguete and Cebu. The port is renowned for its picturesque views of the sea and surrounding hills, making it a welcoming gateway to the island.
