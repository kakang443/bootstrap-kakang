Talingting Port

Talingting Port, located in Barangay Talingting, Enrique Villanueva, Siquijor, is a small but important seaport that serves both local fishermen and inter-island travelers. Nestled on the northeastern coast of the island, the port offers scenic views of the sea, particularly during sunrise, making it a peaceful and picturesque spot for visitors. It plays a vital role in the community by accommodating fishing vessels, small passenger boats, and cargo shipments to nearby islands such as Cebu and Bohol. While its facilities are basic—with simple docking structures and waiting areas—it is well-integrated into the daily lives of locals who rely on the port for both livelihood and transportation. The surrounding waters are rich in marine biodiversity, making the port a hub for sustainable fishing practices. Talingting Port’s quiet charm and authentic coastal atmosphere make it a hidden gem for those seeking a glimpse into Siquijor’s traditional way of life.
